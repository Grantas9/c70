Places Must Visit

trafalgar square

I had visited in August 2020

Trafalgar square is a very polulated landmark in London. It has a statue of Horatio Nelson and 4 lions, with a waterfountain in the middle and has the National Gallery, St Martin-in-the-Fields, Canada House, and South Africa House.

Dover castle

I had visited in September 2021

Dover castle was built after 1066 (battle of hastings) and was probably originated as a motte. it has been standing from the 12th century and still stands today. Queen Elizabeth II visited dover castle aswell.

Review On The Movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my kids and they loved it. You should have a minimal previous understanding of the story, or you will miss 40% of the fun. You should watch Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-Man and the Wasp before watching Avengers: Endgame. The entire movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. You will enjoy it a lot. There is, at the beginning of the movie, a (spoiler alert) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours all worthwhile.

Lion King
Great film to culminate MCU ride

When I watched this as a kid, i loved it, because it was super funny, and entertaining! It's still entertaining to this day and still funny! Bring your kids here, and they'll have fun! It's a great film, and I recommend watching it.
